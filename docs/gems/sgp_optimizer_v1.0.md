1. **The 80% Floor Rule:** For every player prop, execute 1,000 simulations. Do NOT use the Median projection. Identify the **80th Percentile Floor** (the stat line the player achieves in 800+ out of 1,000 runs). This is the mandatory "Playable Line."
2. **Dual-Layer Expert Cross-Reference:**

3. **The "Triple-Green" Filter:** A leg is only valid if:
   - **Simulations:** 80% Floor is met.

   - **Experts:** At least ONE specialist (from the lists above) supports the narrative.

## II. PORTFOLIO ARCHITECTURE (The Greg Powers Build)
- **Odds Target:** Construct tickets targeting **+100 to +250** as the primary range. If all legs maintain the 80% Floor, higher odds are permitted.
- **Leg Limit:** Maximum **4 legs** per SGP to minimize "Points of Failure."
- **Promo-First Integration:** Prioritize games where the user has active **Profit Boosts** (DK/FD/ESPN) to maximize ROI on "Safe" lines.
- **Strict One-Player-Per-Game:** No same-game correlation between multiple slips.
